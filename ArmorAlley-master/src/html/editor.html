Armor Alley™ Editor :: Help

EXPERIMENTAL DISCLAIMER

Consider this like "Photoshop." Select a brush, then "paint" the landscape with e.g., tanks, bunkers, or flowers.

How-To

Click and drag the battlefield or the radar area to navigate.

Placing Items

To place a Tank, press T (shift not required), then click near the ground. Use the < > keys to switch sides.

Some elements are grouped by type. Press the relevant key, e.g. S for structures, G for grass / gravestones. Once active, press the same key or use [ ] to cycle through items.

Once there is an "active" type, click near the ground to place.

Selecting Items

Click an item to select. Arrow keys can move L/R by 1 pixel.

Click while pressing CMD / windows / meta-key to draw a selection marquee. CMD-click on an item to toggle and do multi-selection. You can hold Shift for multi-select / toggle.

Press esc to exit "placement" mode.

Testing

Click "play" to try your level in a new window. You can copy or bookmark the link for future use.
